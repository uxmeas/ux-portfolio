Cookie Policy

Last updated: May 4, 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help site owners understand how their site is used. This portfolio also uses browser local storage, which serves a similar purpose but stays entirely on your device.

Cookies on this site

This portfolio uses analytics cookies only, and only after you grant consent via the cookie banner. No advertising, marketing, or fingerprinting cookies are used.

Cookie Set by Purpose Duration
_ga Google Analytics 4 Distinguishes unique visitors 2 years
_ga_* Google Analytics 4 Maintains session state for the property 2 years
_clck, _clsk, CLID Microsoft Clarity Session replay and heatmap aggregation 1 year

What's not used

  • No advertising cookies
  • No social media tracking cookies
  • No third-party marketing cookies
  • No fingerprinting or cross-site tracking

Local storage

This portfolio stores a small number of preferences in your browser's local storage. This data is never sent to any server and stays entirely on your device.

KeyPurpose
uxmeas-cookie-preferencesYour cookie consent choice (necessary / analytics) and the timestamp it was set

Managing your consent

You can change your analytics consent at any time:

  • Click Cookie Settings in the footer of any page on this site to reopen the consent banner
  • Block or delete cookies through your browser's privacy settings
  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on (browser extension)

Declining analytics will prevent Google Analytics 4 and Microsoft Clarity from loading. The site will continue to function normally.
