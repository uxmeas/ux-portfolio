Deep Problem Framing
The Goal

I designed the deal management platform that investment dealers like Canaccord Genuity and Raymond James use to manage private placements — from offering creation through investor subscription, funding, and distribution. The platform eliminated email-based workflows and reduced deal close time by 48%.

The Problem

Investment dealers managed deals through email chains and spreadsheets. 40% of subscription agreements arrived with errors. ECM teams spent weeks chasing documents instead of closing deals. Canaccord Genuity told us: "Everything was done through email — there was just a lot of room for error and things took way longer than they needed to."

How Might We

How might we replace email-based deal management with a platform that reduces close time by 48% and eliminates 98% of document errors — for dealers managing hundreds of private placements?

Before Email & spreadsheets 40% of subscription docs had errors. Weeks to close. Manual workflows.
After 48% faster close 98% fewer doc errors. $647M facilitated in 2023. 71% YoY growth.
Clients Enterprise dealers Canaccord Genuity, Raymond James, Echelon, Sprott Capital, TSX Trust
User Research
01 Issuers need self-service

Fund managers wanted to launch offerings without waiting for lawyers to draft every document. I designed a guided offering creation wizard that let issuers configure deals independently.

02 Investors distrust opaque processes

When investing $50K-$500K, investors need full transparency into deal structure, compliance status, and portfolio performance. I built dashboards that surfaced this data clearly.

03 Too many handoffs in the deal lifecycle

From offering creation → investor onboarding → compliance → subscription → funding → distributions. Each handoff was a spreadsheet or email. I designed a unified platform that eliminated manual handoffs.

Reflection
✓ What Worked

1. Building the full lifecycle in one platform — eliminated data handoffs and gave all stakeholders a single source of truth

2. Token-based white-labeling — one design system serving 50+ client brands without per-client customization overhead

3. Growing from 3 to IPO with design at the table — proving that UX leadership drives business outcomes in fintech

◈ What I'd Do Differently

1. Invest in a design system earlier — the first 5 years were ad hoc components that created consistency debt

2. Push for dedicated user research earlier — we relied on founder intuition too long before formalizing research
