### Hero
- **Title**: "Turning Word Documents into Figma Designs"
- **Subtitle**: "A Figma plugin that converts .docx files into styled Figma designs. Upload a Word document, pick a style preset, and get auto-layout frames with proper typography hierarchy. Built for designers who inherit content in Word format."
- **Stats**: Seconds (Conversion time) | <10KB (Bundle size) | 7 (Text Styles) | 5 (Wizard Steps)
- **Hero Image**: Plugin UI screenshot placeholder
- **Background**: #0C0C0C

### Section 2 — Challenge
- **Title**: "THE CHALLENGE"
- **Subtitle**: "Why manual DOCX formatting wastes hours"
- **Description**: "Designers inherit content in Word documents. Pasting into Figma means losing all structure. Headings become body text. Lists flatten. Tables disappear. The manual process of reformatting a 20-page document takes hours of tedious, error-prone work."
- **Highlight**: "The gap between Word and Figma was a workflow problem hiding in plain sight."
- **Background**: #161616

### Section 3 — Insight
- **Title**: "THREE DISCOVERIES"
- **Cards**:
  - One-Click > Config: "Designers don't want configuration screens. They want to drop a file and see results. Every setting I removed made the plugin better."
  - Plugin UI Discipline: "A Figma plugin panel is 300px wide. That constraint forced me to rethink every interaction. No sidebars. No tabs. Just a vertical flow."
  - Dual-Layer Parsing: "Word documents have two layers of structure: the visual formatting and the semantic markup. Parsing both gives you accurate Figma output."
- **Background**: #0C0C0C

### Section 4 — The Wizard
- **Title**: "THE WIZARD"
- **Description**: "Five steps from Word document to styled Figma design. Each step handles one clear decision. No configuration screens, no settings panels. Just forward momentum."
- **Steps**: 01 Upload | 02 Preview | 03 Style | 04 Generate | 05 Done
- **Background**: #161616

### Section 5 — Compact Pro
- **Title**: "COMPACT PRO"
- **Subtitle**: "Designing for 480 x 600px"
- **Description**: "Figma plugin panels are 300-480px wide and 600px tall. Every pixel matters. I built a complete design system for this constraint: compact inputs, stacked controls, icon-only toggles, and a type scale that stays readable at 11px."
- **Highlight**: "The constraint became the product's personality. Dense, precise, zero wasted space."
- **Background**: #0C0C0C

### Section 6 — Brand Identity
- **Title**: "BRAND IDENTITY"
- **Description**: "The copper-to-teal gradient is the brand story. Copper represents the raw input. A Word document. Unformatted, unstructured. Teal represents the output. A styled Figma design. Clean, structured, ready to ship. The gradient connects them. Transformation in a single visual."
- **Has**: Color swatches (Copper #D4956A, Teal #2A8F8A, Seafoam #5EDDB8, Dark #0C0C0C, Surface #222222) + gradient bar + website/logo gallery
- **Background**: #161616

### Section 7 — Results
- **Title**: "RESULTS & IMPACT"
- **Intro**: "From hours of manual formatting to seconds of automated conversion. The plugin handles the tedious work so designers can focus on design."
- **Cards**:
  - Seconds — Conversion Time — "What used to take hours of manual formatting now happens in seconds."
  - < 10KB — Bundle Size — "Lean plugin. No bloated dependencies. Fast install, instant load."
