Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences and understand how you use it. Some cookies are essential for the site to work; others help us improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use Google Consent Mode v2 to ensure no analytics cookies are set until you explicitly opt in via the cookie consent banner on this site. By default, all non-essential cookies are blocked.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We do not use any advertising, marketing, or social media tracking cookies. The only third-party cookies come from Google Analytics (GA4), and only when you opt in.

Google Fonts are loaded from Google's servers. While this involves a standard web request (which includes your IP address), Google Fonts does not set cookies.

6. Managing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie icon in the bottom-left corner of any page, or by using the button below:

Browser Controls

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and Site Permissions

7. Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. If your browser sends a DNT header, analytics will not be loaded regardless of your cookie consent settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy as our site evolves. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
