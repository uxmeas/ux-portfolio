02 — CONTEXT

THE PROBLEM WITH
RANDOMIZERS

Every group decision has a dead moment. Someone pulls out their phone, opens a random number generator, taps a button, reads a number. Nobody watches. Nobody remembers.

I wanted that moment to be the best part. MyPick.io turns it into a pixel car race with mechanical failures, a dynamic camera, and 21 sound effects across 5 categories. Cars don't get eliminated. They break down, slow down, then recover with an adrenaline boost. Built from scratch. Zero dependencies. I wanted to own every pixel.
