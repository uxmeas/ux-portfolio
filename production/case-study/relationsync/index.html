About the Product

RelationSync is a mobile app for capturing and reflecting on relationship memories. Built with a friend as a personal project. Voice-first journaling powered by AI summaries, with calendar views and personal insights.

I handle all design and frontend development. 35 screens designed from scratch, 19 reusable components, and a 7-screen interactive onboarding flow.
