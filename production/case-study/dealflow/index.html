FRAGMENTED
INVESTMENT
EXPERIENCE

Katipult's deal flow — from browsing offerings to completing investments — was built incrementally over years. The result was a disjointed experience with inconsistent navigation, buried deal details, and no mobile consideration.

Investors struggled to compare offerings, track portfolio performance, and understand deal terms. The platform needed a unified approach that served both accredited and non-accredited investor personas.

PLATFORM OVERVIEW

END-TO-END
INVESTOR JOURNEY

The redesigned platform connects five core workflows into a single, coherent experience — from deal discovery through investment completion and ongoing portfolio monitoring.

  • Browse and filter active offerings by asset class, risk, and return
  • Deep-dive into deal terms, documents, and issuer profiles
  • Express interest and complete investment commitment
  • Track portfolio performance with real-time dashboards
  • Manage distributions, tax documents, and communications

HOW MIGHT WE

How might we create an investment experience that gives investors confidence to commit capital — by surfacing the right information at the right moment, across any device?

DESIGN
PRINCIPLES

  • Progressive disclosure — reveal complexity only when investors need it
  • Trust signals — transparency in fees, terms, and issuer credibility
  • Mobile parity — every action possible on mobile, not just viewing
  • Smart defaults — pre-fill from investor profile to reduce friction
COMPETITOR ANALYSIS
IMS (Investment Management System)

Strong portfolio tracking but outdated UI with steep learning curve. No mobile experience.

LEGACY · DESKTOP ONLY
NIAD (National Investment Access Directory)

Good deal discovery but limited investor tools. No commitment flow — redirects to external portals.

MARKETPLACE · LIMITED
DealPoint

Modern UI with comparison features but lacks compliance integration. Separate KYC flow required.

MODERN · FRAGMENTED
Parvis

Excellent mobile experience and onboarding. Limited institutional features and reporting capabilities.
