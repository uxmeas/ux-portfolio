THE CHALLENGE

$200 per
failed verification.
50% drop-off at upload.

Drop-off rates exceeded 50% at the document upload step — investors would start KYC, hit a wall of unfamiliar requirements, and abandon. Data showed each failed verification cost $200+ in manual review by compliance teams who had to chase missing documents over email.

Legal teams across 20+ jurisdictions each required different document sets, different identity checks, and different approval workflows. The platform had one rigid flow trying to serve them all. The challenge: design an adaptive onboarding experience that satisfies every regulator while cutting drop-off and verification costs.

DESIGN APPROACH

Research-driven
compliance design

Partnered cross-functionally with compliance officers, legal teams, and real investors to map the full regulatory landscape before designing any screens. Conducted evaluative research with investors across 4 jurisdictions. The Research Table methodology ensured nothing was built on assumption.

HOW MIGHT WE

How might we design a compliance onboarding experience that satisfies regulators across 20+ jurisdictions while feeling simple enough that a first-time investor can complete it without support?
