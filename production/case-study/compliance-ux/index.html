The experience was fragmented across email, fax, and physical mail. Data showed 50% of investors abandoned at document upload. The goal: satisfy regulators in 20+ jurisdictions while cutting drop-off and verification costs.

DESIGN APPROACH

PROGRESSIVE DISCLOSURE FOR REGULATED WORKFLOWS

Partnered cross-functionally with compliance officers, legal teams, and real investors to map the full regulatory landscape before designing any screens. Conducted evaluative research with investors across 4 jurisdictions. The approach prioritized progressive disclosure: showing investors only what they needed at each step, adapting dynamically to their region.
