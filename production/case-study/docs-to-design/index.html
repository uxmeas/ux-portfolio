THE CHALLENGE

The Universal Pain Point

Every designer has received a Word doc, a PDF, or a Google Doc and thought: "Now I have to rebuild all of this in Figma." Content extraction is tedious, error-prone, and eats hours that should go toward actual design work. Designers copy-paste text block by block, reformat headings, recreate tables, and manually place images. The content is already structured in the source document, but that structure gets lost the moment a designer opens a blank frame.

THREE DISCOVERIES

What the Research Revealed

DISCOVERY 01

Structure already exists in the source file

Documents carry semantic hierarchy through headings, lists, tables, and metadata. Designers manually flatten this structure when copy-pasting, then spend time rebuilding it. A parser that preserves document hierarchy eliminates the reconstruction step entirely.

DISCOVERY 02

Brand application is the real bottleneck

Extracting text is only half the problem. The other half is applying the right fonts, colors, and spacing to match brand guidelines. Most designers spend more time on formatting than on content transfer. Baking brand tokens into the generation step removes the bottleneck.

DISCOVERY 03

Figma plugins must be razor-light

Plugin performance directly affects adoption. A plugin that takes more than 2 seconds to open gets abandoned. Staying under 10KB for the bundle, using zero external dependencies, and deferring heavy parsing to Web Workers means the plugin opens instantly and never blocks the Figma canvas.

THE WIZARD

Five Steps, Zero Friction

The entire workflow lives inside a compact 480 x 600px panel. Each step advances with a single action. No modals, no popups, no context switching. The wizard guides designers from raw document to finished layout in under 30 seconds.

01
Upload

Drag and drop any supported file. The parser auto-detects format and extracts structured content, headings, body text, lists, and tables.

02
Preview

See the extracted content mapped into a structured outline. Verify headings, sections, and hierarchy before any design is generated.

03
Configure

Choose your brand theme, layout template, and spacing preferences. Typography, colors, and component styles apply automatically from your brand tokens.

04
Generate

One click creates the full layout on your Figma canvas. Frames, text layers, and auto-layout are all properly structured and named.

05
Refine

The generated design is fully editable native Figma. Adjust, extend, and polish as needed. Nothing is locked or flattened.

COMPACT PRO

Designing for 480 x 600px

Figma plugins live in a constrained panel. Every pixel must earn its place. The interface uses vertical stacking, collapsible sections, and progressive disclosure to fit a five-step wizard into a space smaller than a mobile screen.

Touch targets are sized for precision. Labels are terse but clear. Visual hierarchy relies on spacing and weight, never on color alone. The result is a plugin that feels spacious despite its compact footprint.
