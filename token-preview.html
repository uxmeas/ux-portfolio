Case Study

Investor Onboarding for Private Capital Markets

Role Head of Product Design
Timeline 2014–2025
Team 3 → 30+ employees
Deep Problem Framing
The Goal

Our team redesigned the investor onboarding flow that was causing 60% drop-off at the document upload step. Each failed verification cost $200+ in manual compliance review.

How Might We

How might we reduce investor onboarding from 45 to under 15 minutes while maintaining compliance across 20+ jurisdictions?

Before 45 min 60% drop-off at document upload
After 12 min 85% completion rate
Key Insights
01 Document fatigue kills completion

Investors uploading 6-8 documents in one session abandoned at 60% rate.

02 Trust signals reduce anxiety

Adding encryption badges and progress indicators cut abandonment by 40%.

03 Save-and-resume is essential

Investors could leave mid-flow and return days later without losing progress.

Results
45 → 12 Minutes Onboarding time reduced
85% Completion Investor onboarding rate
$2B+ Processed Total capital volume
✓ What Worked

Progressive disclosure reduced cognitive load — investors completed one step at a time.

◈ What I'd Do Differently

Mobile-first from day one — we retrofitted mobile support years after desktop launch.
